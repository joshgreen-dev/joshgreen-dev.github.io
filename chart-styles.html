Chart style experiments

I ran my benchmark charts through two open image-edit models to see if they could redesign the flat solid fills into something with more texture. Four looks each, two models.

These are decorative restyles, not the real figures. The edit models repaint the image, so some numbers, labels and axis text get garbled or changed. For anything published I would keep the clean charts and only borrow a texture idea from these.
